Michelle's hair was getting long. She was starting college soon. She wanted to cut her hair before college started. She was also going to donate her hair. She had to cut 8 inches to donate it. She decided to cut it even though it would be really very short. She never had her hair short. It was always long. She decided it was time for a change.

After she cut her hair, she felt good. She loved her new look. She felt fresh. It would also be easier to wash and comb her hair. She also felt good about donating her hair.
